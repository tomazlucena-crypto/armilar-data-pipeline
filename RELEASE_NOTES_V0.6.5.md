# Release notes v0.6.5

## China evidence closure

This staging release implements a dedicated official-source audit for China. It does not claim exhaustive unavailability.

The adapter acquires six official NBS resources and distinguishes:

- 2021 household-survey expenditure in eight combined groups;
- the China Statistical Yearbook 2022 table inventory;
- the 2020 product-based input-output table family;
- the aggregate current-price final verification of 2021 GDP;
- two official methodology PDFs preserved without OCR.

The exact matrix receives zero cells. A closed rejection is allowed only when all critical resources are acquired and match reviewed structural markers.
